Welcome

Because we care about the health of our communities and the futureof our environment, our table grapes - including the renowned crimson seedless - are farmed using the most advanced, earth-friendly technologies. Add our enlightened labor practices, and are the recognized gold standard in the industry.

Located in California's fertile San Joaquin Valley, our farms are ideal for growing delicious, organic fruit. From vineyard stewardship to storage and shipping, we make the best use of precious natural resources, and our packaging utilizes recycled, post-consumer content at every opportunity.

About

Delano Farms offers three distinct brands of table grapes for retailers and their customers: The Farm™, which is 100% organically grown, the Green Label™, which is grown on farms awaiting organic certification, and the world-renowned Delano Farms™ label.

Because your customar base relies on you for good quality at reasonable prices, you are well positioned to leverage the sustainable food movement. Selling Delano Farms table grapes brings many benefits.

Work

Equally important, our Primus Labs/Euregap certification affirms our commitment to food safety, environmental health, and worker welfare.

We are well known for providing health benefits and the best working conditions for our workers, including climate-controlled parking facilities. We believe healthy eating starts with healthy farming, and healthy farming begins with healthy farm workers.
